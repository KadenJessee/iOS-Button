Introduction

The objective of this Lab is to for you to demonstrate your understanding of touch events, Buttons, actions and very, very simple Swift programming. You should have completed the Swift study, the study of iOS 14 Getting Started module 3 and the reading on "Creating a tappable button" before attempting this assignment. This is your second programming assignment in this class, so we will summarize the actions you are to take to set up and complete your project. I will explain in detail only those actions you did not see in the study materials or have never done before.

This program displays a button titled "Change". When the user taps the button, the button’s action is to change a text field. The text field begins with an initial value of "Initial" and with each button press, cycles through the four seasons "Winter", "Spring", "Summer" and "Fall" in that order, displaying "Initial" at app startup and only at startup. Note the capitalization of the values. The tests are case-sensitive.

Tap the button a fifth time and the cycle repeats.
